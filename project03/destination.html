Bali, Indonesia

📍 Bali, Indonesia

Nestled in the heart of the Indonesian archipelago, Bali is a world apart — where ancient Hindu temples rise above terraced rice paddies, ceremonies spill into the streets, and the ocean whispers at every turn. Whether you come for surf, spirit, or solitude, the Island of the Gods leaves its mark.

Highlights

  • Explore the sacred Monkey Forest in Ubud
  • Watch sunrise from the rim of Mount Batur
  • Visit the clifftop Uluwatu Temple at dusk
