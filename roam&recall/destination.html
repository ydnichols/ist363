📍 Bali, Indonesia

Nestled in the heart of the Indonesian archipelago, Bali is a world apart — where ancient Hindu temples rise above terraced rice paddies, ceremonies spill into the streets, and the ocean whispers at every turn.

Highlights

  • Explore the sacred Monkey Forest in Ubud
  • Watch sunrise from the rim of Mount Batur
  • Visit the clifftop Uluwatu Temple at dusk
